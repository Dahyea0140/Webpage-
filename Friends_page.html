Friends

Friends is a sitcom about six young adults—Rachel, Monica, Phoebe, Joey, Chandler, and Ross—navigating life in New York City, with a central theme of friendship and how it can be like a chosen family. The series follows their romantic relationships, career struggles, and personal growth over 10 seasons, often centered around the coffee shop Central Perk.
